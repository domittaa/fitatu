What is TER?

TER (Total Energy Requirement) is the number of calories used daily which allow us to maintain, decrease or increase body weight by eating more or less calorific meals. To achieve the desired goal we need to change our diet or physical activity.

What is PAL?

Physical Activity Level (PAL) is a way to express a person's daily physical activity as a single number. If you don't know what is your PAL use a table:

PAL Physical activity Professional activity
1.2 No physical activity. No physical activity. Cerebral palsy patient.
1.4 Light (around 140 minutes weekly) Office worker getting little or no exercise
1.6 Average (around 280 minutes weekly) Office worker, exercising 2/3 times a week for an hour minimum
1.8 High (around 420 minutes weekly) Office worker, exercising 4/5 times a week for an hour minimum
2.0 Very high (around 560 minutes weekly) Proffesional athlete, exercising 6 times a week or a person who does hard manual job
