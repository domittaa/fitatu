What is BMR?

Even when resting, your body burns calories by performing basic functions to sustain life, such as breathing, circulation, nutrient processing or cell production. Basal metabolic rate (BMR) is the number of calories your body needs to accomplish its most basic (basal) life-sustaining functions.
